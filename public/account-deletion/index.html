Delete your account & data

For the Biteaze Billing Android app · Biteaze Solutions LLP

This page explains how to permanently delete your Biteaze Billing account and the data associated with it. You can request deletion in three ways — in the app, here on the web, or by email. You do not need to install or sign in to the app to request deletion via the web or email.

App: Biteaze Billing (Android) · com.aistudio.voicebillingpos.jyxqwt
Published by Biteaze Solutions LLP. See also our Privacy Policy.

1. Delete in the app Fastest · immediate

The quickest way to delete everything is from inside the app:

  1. Open Biteaze Billing and go to More → Profile.
  2. Tap Delete Account.
  3. When prompted, type DELETE to confirm.
  4. Confirm. The app calls our secure deleteAccount function, which permanently erases your account and cloud data.

This is immediate and irreversible. Signing out or switching accounts also wipes the local database from that device.

2. Request deletion on the web No login needed

If you cannot access the app, submit a request on this page. Provide the email address registered to your Biteaze Billing account so we can locate and verify it. We will verify your identity (for example, by confirming control of that email address) and then erase your account and cloud data.

What to include The email (and, if you used phone sign-in, the phone number) registered to your account, and a short note that you want your account and data deleted. Once verified, web-submitted deletions are processed immediately and irreversibly.

If this page does not show a request form, email us using the details in Section 3 below — that is an equally valid deletion channel.

4. What gets deleted

When your deletion request is completed, we erase:

  • Your Firebase Authentication account (your sign-in identity).
  • All of your cloud data at users/{your-account-id}/… and its subcollections — products, orders and line items, expenses, customers, staff, add-ons, variations, and inventory ledger.
  • Your subscription/entitlement document.

Your on-device data is removed when you delete in-app, or when you sign out / switch accounts on a device. If you uninstall the app, the local database is removed with it.

5. What is retained after deletion (and why)

A small amount of data is kept, by design
WhatWhyHow long
Anti-abuse identity hash (in our trial_history ledger) A one-way, non-reversible hash we keep to stop people repeatedly farming our free trial. We cannot reverse it to recover your identity and we do not use it to contact you or build a profile. Retained even after account deletion, to protect the integrity of our trial system.
Records required by law (e.g. certain tax/financial records) We are legally required to retain some records. Only for as long as the applicable law requires.
Apart from these, deletion erases your personal data from our systems. See Section 14 of the Privacy Policy for full detail.

6. Timeline & confirmation

  • In-app and web deletions — processed immediately and irreversibly once confirmed/verified.
  • Email deletions — completed within 30 days of a verified request.
  • Confirmation — we send a confirmation to your registered email address once deletion is complete.
  • Irreversible — account deletion is permanent. There is no undo and no grace period for the account itself. (This is separate from the 90-day read-only grace that applies to an expired subscription — see the Privacy Policy.)

7. Need help?

If you have any question about deleting your account or data, contact our Grievance Officer at support@biteaze.com. We aim to respond within one month (30 days).
